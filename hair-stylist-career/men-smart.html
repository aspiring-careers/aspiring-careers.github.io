To cut hairs for others is a skills that can be learned by following the simple step-by-step instructions given by a teacher. We recommend you to learn from a video tutorial by Chelsea Welechenko.

In this video Chelsea explain and show you how to start cutting for men short that looks smart. First Chelsea explain to you how to cut the hairs with a clipper. She demonstrated how she starts with cutting the back, left and right side with a clipper. This is an easy to follow steps that enable you to learn the basic and the important skills in cutting hairs. Once you learn this basic skills then you become more confident in cutting hairs. In the second part Chelsea will demonstrated to you how to cut hairs with a pair of scissors. The steps given by Chelsea are very direct and easy to follow along. A lot of parents who watch this video start to cut hairs for their children, hubsand and parents as well. We hope you will watch this video a few times and then do it for yourself as a first timer. We believe that you will pick up the simple ways Chelsea teach you how to cut men's hair.
