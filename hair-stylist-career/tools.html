Tools you need to use as a hair stylist

There a few tools you should invest and buy in order to start learning to cut hairs.
1. A pairs of scissors
2. A comb (preferably a plastic comb)
3. A customer salon apron
4. A clipper and a trimmer
5. A thinning shears
6. A few hair clips for clipping hairs to separate the hairs into sections
7. An ultra fine mist water sprayer bottle
You can buy these tools in a supermarket or in Amazon.com or any electronic store near where you live. When you cut hairs with scissors please be careful not to cut your fingers or customer ears. However, cutting hairs with a clipper or trimmer will be safer.
