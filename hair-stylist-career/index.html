A website to provide information and introduce you to the teachers that can help you learn to be a hair stylist

To be a hair stylist as a hobby in your free time or a professsional hair stylist is very rewarding

To cut hairs for others is a skills that can be learned by following the simple step-by-step instructions given by a teacher

In this website we provide information to you to start learning to cut hairs. You can learn from the teachers we recommended to you that include cutting hairs for men and women. Of course you can also apply the cutting skills to cut hairs for children as well. These teachers in their video tutorials provide you with a step-by-step easy to follow along techniques to cut hairs. Below is a picture of a hair stylist cutting hairs. We hope you can read and watch the video tutorials by the teachers. And then start practising what you have learned from the teachers. If you have any comments or feedback please let us know.

In this section, we introduce to you a summary of the key steps by the teacher Norman to cut hairs for women.


In general, all hair cuts have the similar steps to complete the task of cutting hairs. Norman is a professional hair stylist and he is a very good teacher as well. He explains and shows you how to do each of the steps so that you can follow along and learn from him.
